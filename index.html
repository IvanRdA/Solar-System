About:
Our model of the solar system is at scale. It means that distances ratios between planets are equal to reality, as diameter values, orbital speed, etc.
For obvious reasons, it can't be a homogeneous scale because the difference of size and distance between the objects. For that reason, you must know that the proportions are the following:
- Distance from sun = (real distance / 10.000) * 3. For Jupiter is real distance / 10.000. For Saturn is (real distance / 10.000) / 1,5. For Uranus is (real distance / 10.000) / 2 and for Neptune is (real distance / 10.000) / 2,5 - Diameter = - Orbital period =
